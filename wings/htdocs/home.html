wingS is net generation swing

wingS developement was started in the end of 1998 by mercatis. It is a joint effort of several people from mercatis and Thinking Objects. While version 0.9 is by far not feature complete, it is quite usable though. It is already used in several applications and has been heavily tested.

Feel free to contribute! Send patches / fixes / extensions to developer@wings.to.com. Contact Michael Reinsch to get write acces to the CVS Repository.
