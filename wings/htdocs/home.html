wingS is net generation swing

wingS developement was started in the end of 1998 by mercatis information systems gmbh. It is a joint effort of several people from mercatis and Thinking Objects. While the 0.9.x series is by far not feature complete, it is quite usable though. It is already used in several applications and has been heavily tested.

Feel free to contribute! Send patches / fixes / extensions to wings-developers@wings.to.com. Contact Michael Reinsch to get write access to the CVS repository.

There are also some mailing lists for wingS.
